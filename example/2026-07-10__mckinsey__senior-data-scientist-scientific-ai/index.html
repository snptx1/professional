FIVSS · Founder IP Acquired · Booking Holdings

2012 – 2022 · Acquired
FIVSS · predictive-pricing platform for five-star hotel-suite inventory · acquired by Booking Holdings (NASDAQ: BKNG; parent of Booking.com, Priceline, Kayak, OpenTable)

Founded and led FIVSS through a ten-year build, from an original insight — that the secondary market for luxury suite inventory was systematically mispriced — to a proprietary ML pricing engine, a live transactional marketplace, and ultimately a strategic acquisition by Booking Holdings. Operated as sole founder for the majority of the company’s lifespan, owning every function from model design to legal-entity formation to buyer negotiation.

  • Product thesis: Identified persistent inefficiencies in five-star suite pricing across metropolitan and resort markets. Built proprietary demand-forecasting and price-discovery models that flagged underpriced inventory in near-real-time for secondary-market resale.
  • Data & ML: Designed and ran ingestion pipelines against rate-source APIs and structured scrapers; normalized and warehoused millions of price observations in SQL. Trained gradient-boosted and time-series demand models that generated per-suite fair-value estimates and rank-ordered opportunities by expected margin.
  • Platform: Built the full transactional web application — inventory dashboard, buyer-facing UI, payments integration, and back-office reconciliation — as sole engineer for the founding stretch.
  • Commercial operations: Formed the operating entity, negotiated supplier and partner agreements, ran the day-to-day P&L, and grew the business from bootstrapped prototype to a cash-flow-generating operator with recurring inventory relationships.
  • Exit: Led the buyer-side workstream through diligence and close, culminating in the 2022 acquisition by Booking Holdings.
